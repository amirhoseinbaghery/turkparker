About us

Welcome to Viacor!

VIACOR - Live on it! The name reflects the programme. VIACOR (Latin) - "way of the heart" is our guiding star! The fundamental basis of all life!

We work with and for people.

VIACOR -The slogan "Live on it" stands for our claim to give a positive feeling to everyday experiences with the floor: It feels good! It's great to walk, work, drive, play... and live on it.

As a creative workshop and synthetic resin manufacturer for innovation, function and efficiency, VIACOR develops solutions to create positive, unforgettable experiences with floors.

For over 20 years, we develop, manufacture and sell jointless floor coatings and sealers in a thickness from micrometer to centimeter.

Specifically:

1.) Functional floorings for business & industry
2.) PORPLASTIC sport surfaces for Indoor & Outdoor
3.) Waterproofing for special applications

From Germany for the entire world - in all climate zones the world is not enough

VIACOR is the creative workshop + synthetic resine manufactory for innovation, performance and efficiency in superior solutions for floors.

For what reason?

Very simply, because we permanently grow on our duties, to make a contribution in and for one world we want to live and enjoy!"

Dr. Ralf Jooss, CEO

HISTORY ALL-IN

VIACOR FOLLOW THE HEART

In the beginning was the fire. Do what you are passionate about! Follow your own virtues confidently, like developing courage, prudence, cleverness, free spirit and thus shape the future. Creating a living space, being able to be an inspiration to others and standing up for it together. Nothing is as certain as change for the sheer joy of doing.

In freedom, love and yes - adventure, go your own way. Leave your own "impressions". That was the initial spark for me - "in Germany there is no death penalty" - so everyone always wins!

This was followed by permanent further development - consequently - essentially following the CREATIVE streak - constantly developing new products and systems for our customers. This resulted in "protecting values" and inventing "functional surface systems" on which we humans feel comfortable or we can achieve top sporting performance safely and reproducibly.
