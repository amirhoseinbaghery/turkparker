Sport

Win on it.
Count on it.
Live on it.

Winning means that you are willing to run longer, to work harder, and to give more than everyone else [Vince Lombardi - American Football Coach]

Since more than 50 years we develop sports flooring systems. PORPLASTIC sports coatings are being used worldwide in sports clubs / sports centres, for running tracks, sports halls, multifunctional playing fields, fall protection for playgrounds and artificial turf.

Sub-Areas

Tennis Courts

The diversity of tennis court systems! Low-maintenance all-weather sand courts or hard courts for tennis halls or outdoor facilities. Since many years Porsche counts on the PORPLASTIC RedClay pro system for the WTA tournament in Stuttgart.

Running Tracks

Athletic surfaces for stadium, schools and sport centers - Excellent track record as sporting surfaces used in mass sports as well as the optimum surface for pros when performing during top-class level national or international competitions and tournaments.

Sports Halls

The individual layers of the indoor flooring system determine the type of sports for usage. The point elastic systems are ideal for school sports halls and areas used for gymnastics. The combined elastic systems are an ideal system for heavier loads and ball sports.

Play­grounds & Fall Protection

Children’s play areas should be clean, safe and fun. The highly elastic PORPLASTIC impact-absorbing surfaces prevent accidents and protect from injury: smooth surfaces to prevent tripping. Public fitness areas for outdoor workouts must comply with stringent legal requirements concerning the safety aspects of fall protection.

Multi­purpose Playing Fields

The all-rounder for all team sports! Our „All-round sport surfaces“ for playing fields and running tracks are economical possibilities for schools and clubs due to little maintenance effort and since all sports can be practiced on these floors. The ideal sports field for all: soccer, futsal, handball, tennis, school sport, rugby, basketball, volleyball and many more.

Fitness Studio

The power surface for man and machine! The impact of heavy load machines, robust training equipment or people on the ground of a fitness studio could not be different. The floor covering PORPLASTIC ACTIVE Fitness was created especially for these demands. One for All!

Leisure Facilities

Jogging-Tracks, Outdoor Gyms, Skater Parks, Fun Parks and much more. The sporting activities are more diverse than ever before and the requirements for the sports surfaces have strongly increased. The systems of the Active Line offer perfect protection and performance.
